Once Laradock is running, these are the things you'll actually reach for. Each guide is short and task-focused: one problem, the commands to solve it, and the reasoning behind them.

## Start here

If you're new, do these in order: bring up your [containers](/docs/containers), learn how [volumes](/docs/volumes) persist your data, and skim [environment](/docs/environment) so you know where settings live. That's enough to be productive.

## Then reach for the rest as needed

The other guides answer specific questions as they come up, running [multiple projects](/docs/multiple-projects) or [multiple PHP versions](/docs/multiple-php-versions) side by side, [customizing images](/docs/customizing-images), wiring up [Xdebug in your IDE](/docs/xdebug-ide), or [networking](/docs/networking) between containers. You don't need to read them front to back; jump to the one that matches what you're stuck on.
